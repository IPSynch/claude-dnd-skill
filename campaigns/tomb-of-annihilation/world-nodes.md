# Adventure Nodes & Quest Seeds — tomb-of-annihilation

*Lazy reference — NOT loaded at session start. Pull only the current act's nodes when a scene needs them. Exact room/encounter detail lives in the player's copy of the module; this is the structural index.*

## Quest Seed Bank

- **The Charge (main):** The patron (Syndra Silvane, a wizard dying of the curse) hires the party to find and end the Death Curse's source in Chult's interior. Reward: payment + the world's survival + the patron's life.
- **A Guide is Required:** No one crosses Chult's jungle without a guide. Hiring one (Ch.1) is the gate to Ch.2. Each guide carries a hook (Eku=Harper couatl, Qawasha=Emerald Enclave druid, Azaka=weretiger, etc.).
- **Rival Expeditions:** Red Wizards and yuan-ti agents want the party to *find* the Tomb so they can seize it. Some "allies" are spies.
- **Camp Vengeance:** A failing Order of the Gauntlet outpost in the jungle begging for relief — side quest and resupply.
- **Side quests in Port Nyanzaru:** dinosaur race sponsorship, recovering a merchant prince's stolen goods, clearing a haunted villa, etc. (Ch.1 "Things to Do").

## Act 1 Nodes — Port Nyanzaru
- **The Harbor & Red Bazaar** — arrival, rumors, first faction contacts.
- **Merchants' Ward villas** — the seven princes; sponsorship and side quests.
- **Temple District** — Savras (Grandfather Zitembe, divination on the curse), Sune, Tymora, Waukeen.
- **Fort Beluarian** — Flaming Fist garrison under Liara Portyr; charters, contracts, the "Death Curse" bounty angle.
- **Guide auditions** — Azaka Stormfang, Eku, Faroul & Gondolo, Hew Hackinstone, Musharib, Qawasha & Kupalué, River Mist & Flask of Wine, Salida, Shago.

## Act 2 Nodes — The Land of Chult (hexcrawl; any order)
- **Camp Vengeance** — embattled Order of the Gauntlet outpost.
- **Firefinger** — rocky spire, pterafolk lair, ranger's cache.
- **Mbala** — plateau village home to the green hag **Nanny Pu'pu**; dangerous oracle/quest-giver.
- **Kir Sabal** — aarakocra cliff refuge; elder **Asharra**, exiled princess **Mwaxanaré**'s line; can teach feather-fall descent and point to Omu.
- **Orolunga** — ziggurat oracle; the guardian naga **Saja N'baza** reveals the way to Omu and lore of the cubes.
- **Wreck of the Narwhal** — beached ship, Artus Cimber / Ring of Winter thread (**Artus Cimber**, **Dragonbait**).
- **Hrakhamar** — dwarven forge held by firenewts; **Musharib** ties in.
- **Nangalore** — ruined garden-palace, flowers, the frost-giant **Drufi** / Ring of Winter thread.
- **Dungrunglung** — grung kingdom of **Krcaptol**; froghemoth.
- **Wandering threats** — undead, dinosaurs, zombie T-rex rumors (the King of Feathers ranges near Omu).

## Act 3 Nodes — Omu (the nine-shrine hub)
- **The Ruined City** — sinkhole-ringed dead city; yuan-ti patrols, grung, the **King of Feathers** (undead tyrannosaurus) stalking the ruins.
- **The Nine Shrines** (one puzzle cube each; any order): Obo'laka, Ijin, Kubazan, Moa, Nangnang, Papazotl, Shagambi, Unkh, Wongo. Each is a themed puzzle/encounter; the god's spirit may bargain.
- **Nanny Pu'pu / Withers / yuan-ti** may hold cubes the party must pry loose.
- **The Palace / Tomb entrance** — where the assembled cubes open the way down.

## Act 4 Nodes — Fane of the Night Serpent
- **The Yuan-ti Temple** beneath Omu: **Ras Nsi** (exiled war-leader), **Fenthaza** (nightmare speaker scheming to supplant him), the captive princess **Mwaxanaré**, holding cells, the serpent god's sanctum, and the path to the Tomb.

## Act 5 Nodes — Tomb of the Nine Gods (megadungeon, 6 levels)
- **L1 Rotten Halls · L2 Dungeon of Deception · L3 Vault of Reflection · L4 Chambers of Horror · L5 Gears of Hate · L6 Cradle of the Death God.**
- **Withers** — undead servant who can act as macabre guide.
- **The Soulmonger & the atropal** — destroy the artifact, end the godling, lift the curse. **Acererak** looms but rarely fights directly; he escapes to scheme again.
