# NPCs (Full Entries) — tomb-of-annihilation

### Acererak — the Archlich
- **Role:** the architect of the Death Curse; builder of the Soulmonger and the Tomb of the Nine Gods. **CR:** legendary. **Location:** the Tomb (L6), but his presence haunts the whole dungeon.
- **Demeanor:** patient, contemptuous, amused by intruders. **Motivation:** nurture the atropal — a stillborn godling — into a true death-god he controls. The curse is a side effect of harvesting souls to feed it.
- **Secret:** he doesn't defend the Tomb personally; the traps and the trickster-god trials do the work. He'd rather let intruders die than soil his hands, and will flee his phylactery elsewhere if truly threatened.
- **Speech:** unhurried, grandiose, dismissive — speaks to mortals as a man addresses insects he designed.
- **Relationships:** created **Withers**; despises the **trickster gods** (whom he murdered); indifferent to the **yuan-ti** squatting above his Tomb.
- **Attitude toward party:** hostile.

### Syndra Silvane — the Dying Patron (default quest-giver)
- **Role:** a wizard wasting away from the Death Curse who hires the party to end it. **Location:** Port Nyanzaru.
- **Demeanor:** failing but resolute; every day visibly costs her. **Motivation:** survive — and spare everyone else her fate. She is the party's living clock.
- **Secret:** Harper-aligned; her urgency is real and her resources finite.
- **Speech:** measured, tired, occasionally wry about her own decline.
- **Relationships:** sympathetic to the **Harpers**; will connect the party to allies in the city.
- **Attitude toward party:** allied. *(Swap in a different patron/hook if the table prefers — e.g. a PC who was once raised and is now dying.)*

### Ras Nsi — Exiled Yuan-ti War-Leader
- **Role:** ancient yuan-ti general ruling the cult beneath Omu. **Location:** Fane of the Night Serpent.
- **Demeanor:** imperious, paranoid, ruthless. **Motivation:** claim the Tomb's power and the atropal's promise; he believes it his due.
- **Secret:** he is cursed (his undead obey him only so long as he stays in Chult); his grip on the cult is slipping as **Fenthaza** plots against him.
- **Speech:** cold command; assumes obedience; sibilant menace.
- **Relationships:** rival of **Fenthaza**; jailer of **Mwaxanaré**; serves the Night Serpent (Dendar).
- **Attitude toward party:** hostile (but exploitable via Fenthaza's coup).

### Fenthaza — Yuan-ti Nightmare Speaker
- **Role:** ambitious priestess plotting to overthrow Ras Nsi. **Location:** Fane.
- **Demeanor:** seductive, calculating, patient. **Motivation:** seize leadership of the cult by using the party as her weapon against Ras Nsi.
- **Secret:** she'll offer the party a deal (cubes/passage for killing or deposing Ras Nsi) and betray them the instant it suits her.
- **Speech:** honeyed, conspiratorial, never quite honest.
- **Relationships:** rival of **Ras Nsi**; commands cult factions loyal to her.
- **Attitude toward party:** hostile but transactional — the campaign's best "deal with the devil."

### Withers — Servant of the Tomb
- **Role:** an undead caretaker who can lead the party into and through the Tomb. **Location:** Omu / the Tomb.
- **Demeanor:** dry, cryptic, unnervingly helpful. **Motivation:** ambiguous — he ushers intruders toward the Soulmonger, but whose ends he truly serves is unclear.
- **Secret:** his guidance is real but never disinterested; he may be steering events toward the atropal's birth — or against it.
- **Speech:** clipped, archaic, riddling.
- **Relationships:** made by **Acererak**; keeper of journals/handouts that warn and mislead.
- **Attitude toward party:** ambiguous.

### Liara Portyr — Commander of Fort Beluarian
- **Role:** Flaming Fist officer running the Baldurian garrison outside Port Nyanzaru. **Motivation:** advance the Flaming Fist's (and her own) hold on Chult.
- **Secret:** she'll charter the party for her own ends and isn't above expedient cruelty.
- **Speech:** brisk, military, transactional.
- **Attitude toward party:** neutral-suspicious.

### Artus Cimber & Dragonbait
- **Artus Cimber** — human explorer bearing the **Ring of Winter**, hunted across Chult by **Drufi**'s frost giants and the cult that wants the ring. Wary but decent; a potential ally and information source.
- **Dragonbait** — saurial paladin, Artus's silent, honorable companion; detects evil and reads intent. Loyal beyond question.
- **Attitude toward party:** neutral-friendly.

### Saja N'baza — Guardian Naga of Orolunga
- **Role:** ancient naga oracle atop the Orolunga ziggurat. **Motivation:** guard old Omu lore; aid worthy seekers.
- **Function:** the cleanest source for *where Omu is* and *what the puzzle cubes are*. Will test and teach.
- **Speech:** serene, scholarly, vast patience.
- **Attitude toward party:** neutral-friendly.

### Asharra & Mwaxanaré (Kir Sabal)
- **Asharra** — aarakocra elder of the cliff refuge Kir Sabal; can teach a feather-fall descent and point toward Omu. Wise, protective.
- **Mwaxanaré** — princess of the old Omuan royal line, later captured by the yuan-ti; a restoration thread that pays off in the Fane.
- **Attitude toward party:** neutral-friendly / sympathetic.

### Nanny Pu'pu — Green Hag of Mbala
- **Role:** a green hag posing as a helpful grandmother. **Motivation:** feed, deceive, and profit. May hold a **puzzle cube** and bargain it away at a cruel price.
- **Secret:** everything she offers has a hook; deals with her cost more than they seem.
- **Speech:** doting, sing-song, menace under the sweetness.
- **Attitude toward party:** hostile-ambiguous.

### The King of Feathers — Undead Tyrannosaurus
- **Role:** an undead T-rex haunting Omu's ruins; far cannier than its bulk suggests. **Function:** the city's roaming apex threat — telegraph it before it strikes.
- **Attitude toward party:** hostile.

### The Seven Merchant Princes of Port Nyanzaru *(summary — expand on contact)*
- **Ekene-Afa** — sells weapons, shields, canoes, travel gear; athlete-hero of the people.
- **Ifan Talro'a** — beast merchant; dinosaurs and mounts.
- **Jessamine** — plants, poisons, and quiet assassinations; veiled and deadly.
- **Jobal** — hires out guides and mercenaries; smug gatekeeper of expeditions.
- **Kwayothé** — food, wine, oil, perfume; gracious and shrewd.
- **Wakanga O'tamu** — the only mage among them; magic items and information; a natural Harper-style ally.
- **Zhanthi** — stone, textiles, and clothing; proud and traditional.
- **Attitude toward party:** neutral — patrons and side-quest givers, each with an agenda.

### Guides (hireable in Port Nyanzaru) *(expand the one the party picks)*
- **Eku** — disguised couatl; the wisest, kindest guide (Harper-aligned celestial). **Qawasha & Kupalué** — Emerald Enclave druid and vegepygmy companion. **Azaka Stormfang** — weretiger with a personal goal at Nangalore. **Musharib** — albino dwarf tied to Hrakhamar. **Faroul & Gondolo**, **Hew Hackinstone**, **River Mist & Flask of Wine**, **Salida**, **Shago** — competent local guides, each a potential thread.

### The Nine Trickster-God Spirits *(add full entries as shrines come up)*
Obo'laka (order/anxiety), Ijin (change), Kubazan (courage), Moa (loyalty), Nangnang (greed), Papazotl (pride), Shagambi (wisdom), Unkh (indecision), Wongo (madness). Each can possess a willing host who solved its shrine, granting a boon and a personality quirk — and each bargains in character.
