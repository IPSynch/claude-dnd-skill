## ⚙ REFLAVOR ENGINE — Spatial / Dimensional Manipulation
*Her mechanics are stock; her **manifestation** is space-folding and reality-editing. Improvise within this register every time — vary it, never repeat the same image twice in a scene. Tag the true mechanic OOC when reflavoring: `(OOC: Shield)`. **Never** name the inspirations in prose.*

**Signature gestures:** a *snap of her fingers*; a lazy flick; arms crossed under her bare chest with a smirk while something impossible happens to the space around her; the **living-graffiti** look — a person or object flattened into a surface (ground, bark, stone) as a moving 2-D image before it pops back into three dimensions.

**Defense / dodge:**
- **Shield (+5 AC) / a missed attack:** the space the blow must cross becomes infinitely divisible — the strike *slows asymptotically and never arrives*. She doesn't flinch. Smirk, arms folded. OR she snaps and **shunts the attack/projectile into the ground or a tree** as a smear of living graffiti. OR she **flattens herself into the nearest surface** for half a second — a sliding painted figure — and peels back out untouched.
- **Warped Space (reaction, 1 SP):** she folds the attack's path into a surface as graffiti and is suddenly **10 ft away**, as if she'd been standing there the whole time.
- **Spell Siphon:** an incoming spell hits the divisible space and *spirals inward into her* — absorbed, leaving her warmer, fuller (temp HP).

**Offense / control:**
- **Turbulent Force:** the ground under the target goes **mush for half a second, then snaps rigid around their shins**, then *launches them 5 ft back* as it releases. OR she snaps and the **space around them tightens like a fist** — an invisible blow to the face, knocked back.
- **Tremor:** she alters the **density and footing** of the ground in the emanation — it sucks at boots, heaves, sweeps legs out from under. Bodies drop prone into terrain that won't hold still.
- **Thunderwave:** she snaps and the **cube of space violently decompresses** — everything inside hurled to the edges.
- **Dragon's Breath:** her palm makes the air's **friction fizzle and the Weave smack against itself** in a 15-ft cone — the ambient magic detonates outward as the chosen element.
- **Sorcerous Burst / Chromatic Orb:** she pinches off a **marble of folded space/element** and flicks it; it *unfolds on impact*.
- **Sacred Flame:** she opens a **hairline seam** above the target to somewhere blindingly bright; light falls through and ignores cover.
- **Mind Sliver:** she presses a **sliver of wrong geometry** into their skull — their own thoughts briefly fold the wrong way.
- **Misty Step:** she **slides sideways into the graffiti-dimension** — a flat painted figure skating across a surface — and steps back out 30 ft away.
- **Silvery Barbs:** she **folds the last half-second of causality** so the enemy's success "never quite happened," and hands the seam's favor to an ally.
- **Innate Sorcery (active):** space **visibly warps around her** — light bends, edges of the world shimmer at arm's length — for the minute it lasts.
- **Cure Wounds:** she **pinches the torn edges of a wound and folds them shut.**
- **Cling to Life:** when she should fall, the space around her **refuses to let her go** — stitched back into the world by something that needs a nearby death to do it again.
