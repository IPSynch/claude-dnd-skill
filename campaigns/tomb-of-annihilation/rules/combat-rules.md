# ⚔ COMBAT RULES — tomb-of-annihilation

> **READ THIS BEFORE EVERY COMBAT.** Player's standing combat conventions. (Companions: `house-rules.md` for rolling/resources, `narration-style.md` for prose.)

## Crit rule (attack crits AND save-fails) — NOT "double dice"
- **Critical hit (nat 20 to-hit) OR nat-1 failed save = MAXIMUM damage dice + a fresh roll of those dice + all flat bonuses.**
  - e.g. a `1d10+6` crit = **10 + 1d10 + 6**. An `8d6` Fireball on a nat-1 save = **48 + 8d6**.
- **Nat 20 on a save = 0 damage / no effect.** Normal success = half. Normal fail = full. Nat-1 save = crit (above), full effect.

## Range — ALWAYS in feet
- State distances in **feet** in narration and the combat table so spell/attack/aura ranges are unambiguous (melee = 5 ft; "the spawn 5 ft off," "across the 10 ft," etc.).

## HP-as-prose for enemies (never numbers in prose)
- >75% **Healthy** · 51–74% **Harmed** · 26–50% **Bloodied** · <25% **Critical.** Narrate look + behaviour to match.

## Pause on a hit to a PC
- When an NPC attack meets/exceeds a PC's AC, **stop before damage** so the player may use a reaction (Shield, Silvery Barbs, etc.).

## Enemy AI tags
- `(Intelligent Enemy: …)` strategizes; `(Unintelligent Enemy: …)` blunders. Tag intentions in the table.

## roll_mode = auto + resource gate
- I roll PC no-resource checks and all NPC dice openly, full breakdown. **NEVER spend a PC's slots / limited features without explicit permission** — suggest instead. Cantrips are free.

## Declare & detail every roll
- Whose roll · what type (save/check/attack) · DC and whose · each die value · total · pass/fail · damage rolled die-by-die · final after save/resist/vuln.

## Combat table EVERY combat response
- Columns: Init · Participant · Slots/Features · HP · Status · Intention · Range. Mark current turn (▶). Below it: **Stakes · Location · Time.**
- **RESOURCE TABLE — REQUIRED in every combat response.** Per PC, track & show: spell slots by level (left/max), limited-use features, current HP, active concentration. Keep it current as things are spent and **carry it across the whole session — no long rest means no reset.**

## Reflavor freely
- Reflavor any non-named statblock/spell/item; tag `(OOC: actual statblock/spell)`. Never reflavor named NPCs.

## ToA-specific combat notes
- **The jungle and the Tomb do the killing.** Traps, disease, exhaustion, and terrain are combatants — narrate footing (mud, roots, flooded stone), heat, and the press of the canopy.
- **Undead & the Death Curse:** resurrection magic fails campaign-wide. A PC who drops is in real danger — no easy raises. Make death weigh.
- **Telegraph the death-traps** before they kill (per the module's design and the style guide's "foreshadow danger" rule). Lethal, but fair.

## CHARACTER BINDINGS

### Limenra — Sorcerer 3 (Limitless Sorcery) — full sheet: `characters/limenra.md`
- **AC 18, no armor** (Untouchable: 10 + DEX + CHA). She fights nude — never assume armor.
- **Spell save DC 16 / spell attack +8.** With **Innate Sorcery** active (1 min, 2/LR): **DC 17 + advantage on spell attacks.**
- **REACTIONS (offer these when she's hit — per "pause on a hit to a PC"):**
  - **Shield** (1st-level slot) → +5 AC, may turn a hit into a miss.
  - **Warped Space** (costs **1 SP**) → negates the hit entirely + teleport 10 ft.
  - **Silvery Barbs** (1st-level slot) → after an enemy hits/succeeds.
  - **Spell Siphon** (2/LR, CHA check) → only vs a spell targeting only her.
  - **Feather Fall** (reaction) when falling.
- **LIMITED-USE RESOURCES — §4 gate; NEVER spend without explicit go-ahead, only suggest:**
  - Spell slots: 1st ×4, 2nd ×2 · Sorcery Points ×3 · Innate Sorcery 2/LR · Spell Siphon 2/LR · Cure Wounds free 1/LR · Strixhaven free 1/LR · Cling to Life (recharges only on a humanoid death within 30 ft).
  - **Cantrips are FREE** (Sorcerous Burst, Turbulent Force, Mind Sliver, Sacred Flame, Prestidigitation, Thaumaturgy) — use at will when fitting.
  - **Warped Space costs 1 SP** — it's a *resource*, so suggest-and-wait unless she's pre-authorized it for the fight.
- **CON saves: advantage** (Adamant Endurance). **All saves vs spells/magic: advantage** (One with the Weave).
- **REFLAVOR — every spell/feature manifests as spatial/dimensional manipulation.** Improvise per the Reflavor Engine in her sheet; vary it; tag the real mechanic OOC, e.g. `(OOC: Shield)`.
- **🚫 OOC-ONLY REFLAVOR KEYS — HARD BAN in prose:** the inspirations behind her kit (the Limitless/Infinity/Hollow-Purple sorcerer; the Hero-X dimension/reality manipulator) are reference keys for the DM ONLY. **Never name them in narration.** Show the effect — folding space, the living-graffiti slide, the infinite slowing, the finger-snap — never the source.
- **Personal items (Rectum of Holding / Shatdora / Cumdora):** Bag-of-Holding-equivalent storage + secretion shunts. Flavor/utility, no combat function unless she weaponizes a purge — adjudicate ad hoc in the committed grotesque register.
