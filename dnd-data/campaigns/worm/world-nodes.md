## Quest Seed Bank

Hooks the protagonist can pursue, grouped loosely by where they surface in the canonical timeline. The protagonist occupies Taylor's structural role, so the early hooks center on the debut and the Undersiders.

**Early (Arcs 1–6):**
- **First night out.** A solo patrol meant to prove the protagonist can do good ends with them face-to-face with Lung, the most dangerous cape in the city. (Inciting incident.)
- **The offer.** After the Lung fight, a team of young villains — the Undersiders — extends a hand. Joining is the path in; the protagonist's motives for accepting are theirs to define (undercover hero? nowhere else to go? curiosity?).
- **The bank job.** The Undersiders' first heist together — a test of trust and a collision with the Wards.
- **Who's the boss?** The team answers to a hidden patron. Finding out who, and what they really want, is a slow burn.
- **The undercover question.** If the protagonist took the Wards/Protectorate path internally, do they report in? To whom? At what risk?

**Mid (Arcs 7–17):**
- **Bakuda's campaign.** The ABB's bomb-tinker turns the city into a minefield; the protagonist is forced to act against escalating terror.
- **Leviathan.** An Endbringer comes for Brockton Bay. Every cape, hero or villain, fights together or dies. The city will not be the same after.
- **Coil's game.** The patron's true plan for the city surfaces; the protagonist must decide what to do about it.
- **The Nine.** The Slaughterhouse Nine arrive and put the protagonist on a list. Survival, defiance, and hard choices.

**Late (Arcs 18–30):**
- **Warlord.** Holding territory and protecting people in a broken city — power used as responsibility, with all its compromises.
- **The truth.** Cauldron, the Endbringers' nature, and Scion. The mystery resolves into the largest stakes possible.
- **The last plan.** The desperate, costly endgame against an unstoppable threat.

## Adventure Nodes (locations)

**Brockton Bay — core:**
- **The Docks / Trainyard** — abandoned industrial waterfront; the Undersiders' early stomping ground and lair territory; gang no-man's-land.
- **The Boardwalk** — the one nice part of town; upscale shops, open-air, patrolled. Capes avoid open conflict here.
- **Downtown** — businesses, the PRT building, the bank (Arc 2 heist site).
- **The PRT Building & Protectorate HQ** — the heroes' base; HQ sits offshore under a force field, reached from the Rig.
- **Lord Street / commercial core** — Empire 88's economic heart.
- **The ferry & abandoned ferry terminal** — shut down; symbol of the city's decline; relevant terrain later.
- **Lung's territory (ABB)** — the Asian district under the ABB's protection/extortion.
- **The Merchants' ground** — the worst-off neighborhoods, drug territory.
- **Arcadia High** — the "nice" school; where Wards and certain civilians cross paths.
- **Winslow High** — the rough school; relevant to the protagonist's civilian life (adapt to the new origin).

**Beyond Brockton Bay (later arcs):**
- **Other Earths** — parallel worlds become reachable and important mid-to-late.
- **Endbringer attack sites** — cities elsewhere that fall; the war is global.
- **Cauldron's domain** — a hidden place outside normal geography.
